Manual therapy uses hands-on techniques — joint mobilization, soft tissue work, and guided stretching — to reduce pain, ease stiffness, and restore normal movement patterns.

It's often used alongside exercise-based rehabilitation, not as a replacement for it, so gains made in a session are supported by a plan you can continue between visits.

## What a session may include

- A movement and pain assessment
- Joint and soft tissue mobilization
- Guided stretching and posture correction
- Take-home exercises to maintain progress

Your exact plan is determined by your physiotherapist after an initial assessment.
