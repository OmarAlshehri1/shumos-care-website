Recovering well after surgery depends on the right movement, at the right time. Post-surgical rehabilitation is paced to your procedure and your surgeon's guidance, supporting healing while gradually restoring strength and function.

Plans are coordinated around any precautions from your surgical team, so you can recover with confidence rather than guesswork.

## What a session may include

- Review of your surgical history and precautions
- Gentle, guided range-of-motion work
- Progressive strengthening as healing allows
- Ongoing monitoring and plan adjustments
