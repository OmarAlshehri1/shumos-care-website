Sports rehabilitation focuses on recovering from athletic injuries safely — and rebuilding the strength, mobility, and confidence needed to return to your sport.

Programs are staged, moving from pain and swelling management through to sport-specific conditioning, so return-to-play decisions are based on how your body is actually responding, not a fixed timeline.

## What a session may include

- Injury assessment and movement screening
- Progressive strength and mobility work
- Sport-specific conditioning drills
- Guidance on a safe return-to-play timeline
