Living with long-term pain calls for a different approach than treating a single injury. Chronic pain management combines hands-on treatment, movement strategies, and education to help you manage symptoms and stay active.

The goal is a sustainable plan — one that fits your daily life, not just what happens in the clinic.

## What a session may include

- A detailed history and pain assessment
- Manual therapy for symptom relief
- A graded movement and exercise plan
- Practical strategies for daily activity and flare-ups
