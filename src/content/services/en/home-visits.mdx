For patients who find it difficult to travel to the clinic, home visits bring physiotherapy care to you — with the same assessment and treatment quality as an in-clinic session.

This option is especially useful during early post-surgical recovery or for patients with limited mobility.

## What a session may include

- A full assessment in your home environment
- Hands-on treatment and guided exercise
- Practical advice suited to your home setup
- A plan for transitioning to clinic visits, if appropriate
