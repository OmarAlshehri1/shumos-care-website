title: "Pediatric Physiotherapy"
summary: "Gentle, specialized care for younger patients."
icon: "baby"
---

Children move, heal, and respond to treatment differently than adults. Pediatric physiotherapy is adapted to a child's age, development, and comfort — kept gentle, engaging, and paced to what they can manage.

Parents and caregivers are involved throughout, with guidance on how to support progress at home.

## What a session may include

- An age-appropriate assessment
- Play-based movement and exercise
- Gentle hands-on techniques where appropriate
- Guidance for parents on home support
